Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is it really free?

Yes! We build your website completely free of charge. No upfront costs, no hidden fees. We make our money through optional premium services like hosting upgrades and ongoing maintenance, but the website itself is 100% free.

What's the catch?

There's no catch! We believe every UK business deserves a professional online presence. We offer optional paid services for those who want extras, but the core website is genuinely free.

How long does it take?

Most websites are completed within 7-14 days from our initial consultation. We work quickly without compromising on quality.

Do I own the website?

Yes! You own all the content and have full control over your website. We'll provide you with all the necessary access and credentials.

What about hosting?

We include basic hosting for the first year free. After that, you can choose to continue with our affordable hosting plans or move to any hosting provider you prefer.
